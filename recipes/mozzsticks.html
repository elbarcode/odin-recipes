How to make mozzarella sticks from cratch

Delicious mozzarella sticks recipe to die for:

This one's for all the people needing a good appetizer to have while watching the game with friend. We created the perfect and easy to make mozzarella sticks recipe that will impress everyone with it's amazing flavor. Follow the steps and let me know how you did!

Ingredients you'll need:

  • 1/2 cups of plain breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 tsp of Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp of dried parsley
  • 1/4 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp of table salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 lb of mozzarella cheese
  • As needed vegetable oil
  • For serving have marinara sauce

Step by step:

  1. Prep cheese: Cut mozzarella sticks in half and freeze them until firm.
  2. Set up dredging: Prepare three bowls: flour, beaten eggs, and seasoned breadcrumb mixture.
  3. Coat in flour: Roll each frozen cheese stick in flour to lightly cover.
  4. Dip in egg: Transfer to the egg mixture so the coating sticks.
  5. Bread the sticks: Roll in the breadcrumb mixture until fully coated.
  6. Double coat: Repeat egg + breadcrumbs for a thicker, crispier crust.
  7. Freeze again: Freeze the coated sticks so they don’t melt too fast when frying.
  8. Heat oil: Warm oil in a pot until hot enough for frying.
  9. Fry sticks: Cook a few at a time until golden brown and crisp.
  10. Drain and serve: Place on paper towels and serve with marinara.
