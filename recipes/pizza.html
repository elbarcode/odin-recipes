How To Make A Pizza From Cratch

Easy steps to make a pizza at home:

Have you ever wished to make the perfect pizza from your home? This is the recipe for you. We'll be explaining step by step how to create the perfect pizza for any occasion.

Ingridients you'll need:

  • One cup of flour
  • Half a tablespoon of yeast
  • A pinch of salt
  • Half a tablespoon of sugar
  • One tablespont of vegetable oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni (optional)

Step by step:

  1. Activate yeast: In a bowl, combine yeast, sugar, and warm water. Stir gently and let sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt
  3. Combine wet and dry: Add the yeast mixture and olive oil into the well and stir until the dough begins to come together.
  4. Knead dough: Transfer to a lightly floured surface and knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Let dough rise: Place in a greased bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size.
  6. Punch down dough: Punch down to release air. Divide into 2 for thin crust or keep whole for thick crust.
  7. Roll out dough: Roll on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  8. Transfer to sheet: Move the dough onto a parchment‑lined baking sheet.
  9. Preheat and rest: Preheat oven to 425°F and let the dough rest for 15 minutes.
  10. Add toppings and bake: Add your favorite toppings and bake for 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
