Making the best vegan brownies

The best and only vegan brownie recipe you'll need:

I feel everybody needs to be able to enjoy a good brownie. So, for all my lactose intolerant friends I'm going to show you the best vegan brownies you"ll ever make. Follow the steps bellow and let me know how they come out!

Ingredients you'll need:

  • 2 tbsp of ground flax seed
  • 5 tbsp of water (for flax egg)
  • 1/2 cup of water (for simple syrup)
  • 1 1/2 cups of vegan cane sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup of dutch-processed cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp of espresso powder
  • 3/4 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup of vegan chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup of chopped walnuts (optional)

Step by step:

  1. Prep the pan: Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C). Line an 8×8‑inch pan with parchment paper and lightly grease.
  2. Make the flax egg: Whisk ground flax seed with 5 tbsp water in a small bowl. Set aside to thicken.
  3. Make the simple syrup: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine cane sugar and 1/2 cup water. Heat 6–8 minutes until sugar fully dissolves. Do not boil.
  4. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, espresso powder (if using), and salt.
  5. Combine wet and dry: Pour the hot simple syrup into the dry mixture. Add oil, vanilla, and the flax egg. Stir gently until just combined — careful to not overmix.
  6. Add mix-ins: Fold in walnuts (if using) and 1/2 cup of the chocolate chips.
  7. Bake: Spread batter evenly into the pan. Top with remaining chocolate chips. Bake 35–40 minutes. Toothpick will not come out clean — brownies are fudgy.
  8. Cool completely: Let brownies cool fully in the pan before slicing (2–3 hours). For firmer brownies, chill 30 minutes before cutting.
